Guoqing Gong (he/him) was a grad student in the Hydroclimatology Group. Born and raised in China, his love for nature inspired him to become an environmental science researcher. He is particularly interested in the attribution and impacts of extreme weather and climate events. His research has focused on investigating trends and mechanisms of drought, using a diverse skill set that includes statistical analysis and machine learning. He holds a MS from [Southern University of Science and Technology](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/en/), China, and a BE from [Fujian Normal University](https://www.fjnu.edu.cn/english/main.htm), China.
